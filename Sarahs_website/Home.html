Sarah Hoch


Sarah Hoch ... smolders as Joanna, the show’s femme fatale.

                                                                                   - Chris Davis, The Memphis Flyer

 
 

Sarah Hoch is a singer, actor, and dancer currently residing in Chicago after finishing up a year as an associate company member of Playhouse on the Square in Memphis, TN. After having recently earned her BFA in Musical Theatre from Drake University in Des Moines, IA, in May of 2011, she moved to Memphis in July of that year to begin a year long contract as an actor on the Playhouse stage. She stayed in Memphis until late July of 2012, then moved to Chicago. She later plans to move to Fort Collins, CO to play "Vivienne" in Legally Blonde at Midtown Arts Center from February to May of 2013. Feel free to explore the website to learn more about Sarah's upcoming performances, past credits, and education, to check out photos of her past work, and to listen to a few sound clips of her performing some of her repertoire.
